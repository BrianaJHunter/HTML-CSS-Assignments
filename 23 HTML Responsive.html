>Kingston

Kingston is the capital of Jamaica.

The most populated city is home to one out of two of the island's airports.

>Montego Bay

Often referred to as "MoBay" for short.

There are caves, ports, beautiful beaches, luxury resorts, golf courses, and the island's remaining airport.

>Ocho Rios

This bustling city is often referred to as "Ochie" for short.

It is the center of the Greater Tokyo Area, and the most populous metropolitan area in the world.
